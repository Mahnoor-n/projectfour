AL SAFAH SCHOOLING SYSTEM

Established in November 1975 as the Les Anges Montessori Academy for toddlers, AL SAFAH SCHOOLING SYSTEM has since grown into a global network of private schools, institutes, and universities, imparting distinctive and meaningful education to students from pre-school to post-graduation. AL SAFAH SCHOOLING SYSTEM, has completed more than sixteen years of service to educational development across the country with remarkable success. Established in 2002, it now has 20+ campuses & the market leader in Pakistan's educational landscape with a presence in over 5+ cities and a student strength exceeding 5,000+. This makes AL SAFAH SCHOOLING SYSTEM outreach the largest compared to any other private school in the world.

Admission procedure

There is no written test for pre-school. The admission test for primary and secondary school is based on English , Urdu Mathematics & Science
